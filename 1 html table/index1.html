The lion (Panthera leo) is one of the most iconic and majestic animals in the animal kingdom. Known as the "King of the Jungle," lions are not typically found in jungles but thrive in savannas, grasslands, and open woodlands. They are native to Africa and a small population exists in India. The one-horned rhinoceros, also known as the Indian rhinoceros (Rhinoceros unicornis), is a remarkable species found mainly in the grasslands and forests of the Indian subcontinent. It is celebrated for its distinctive single horn and armor-like skin, giving it a prehistoric appearance. The panda, or giant panda (Ailuropoda melanoleuca), is a beloved bear species known for its distinctive black-and-white fur and peaceful nature. Native to China, pandas are a global symbol of conservation and are adored for their playful, gentle demeanor.
